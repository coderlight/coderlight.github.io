Making life simpler.

Doing something, about the silly everyday obstacles to simplicity

". . .But having grown to more than 600 workers, the company is facing a new problem: no one can remember who sits where."

Source: The Wall Street Journal (october 8, 2013)
Office Canvass takes a simple approach to solve a silly problem that we all have from time to time, whether we’re a new-employee looking for the restroom, we’re looking for a colleague that moved their desk, or that conference room that is in an area we don’t often explore. Our application provides you with a user experience that is straight forward, give it a try.
Where does that person sit, again?
Only know part of a person’s name - we’ve got you covered. Just type part of the person’s name and we’ll display a call out where they sit, plus bring up useful information if they happen to be out of the office.
Can’t recall a name?
Sometime you don’t recall the name of the person who sits by near you, but you’ve already asked them 3-times what their name is? Well, just hover over the location where they sit and you’ll be given their name, simple, just like that.
Security
We are a cloud application built using secure technologies, such as Amazon Web Services, so there’s no need to stress about your data. Your information won’t be shared or sold to marketer to make a quick buck. Our aim is to provide you with a simple, reliable and secure tool that your organization can use.

Why should you care?

People work with people, and providing those that work with you a simple experience to reduce the small frustration of finding that pinter name near Bob’s desk, or getting a reminder of the person’s name that sits near so-and-so, makes it a bit easier to get things done. Whether you’re a professor giving a lecture in a classroom, a facilities manager of a large complex, or someone in an office just trying to find a printer - this application can help, give it a try.
